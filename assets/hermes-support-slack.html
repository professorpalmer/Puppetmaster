What Puppetmaster brings to me
🧠

Cost-aware routing

Every task lands on the cheapest sufficient model, with a per-decision audit trail and a savings ledger. Pin a model only when you mean to.

🕸️

Swarm fan-out

Audits, reviews and "find all X" run as independent parallel workers in isolated worktrees — no context flooding, no clobbered edits.

📈

CodeGraph context

Workers get task-relevant graph context injected before the model call — symbols, callers, blast radius — instead of grepping blind.

💾

$0 follow-ups

Results persist as typed artifacts in SQLite. Re-read any past job for free — zero tokens, across sessions.

🔁

Self-healing

Provider down or model unavailable? It reroutes to a funded alternate and escalates low-confidence work automatically.

🧾

Durable & auditable

Fail-closed jobs, replayable runs, approval gates before edits. Aviation-grade rigor for handing it your repo.
